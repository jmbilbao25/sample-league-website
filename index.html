Classic Champions

A look back at three of League of Legends' most iconic original champions — the warriors, archers, and mages that defined the early days of the Rift.

Garen

The Might of Demacia

A proud and noble warrior of the Dauntless Vanguard. Clad in magic-resistant armor and bearing a mighty broadsword, Garen stands ready to confront all who threaten Demacia.

Ashe

The Frost Archer

Iceborn warmother of the Avarosan tribe, Ashe commands the most populous horde in the north. She wields a bow of True Ice, hoping to unify the Freljord once more.

Annie

The Dark Child

Dangerous, yet disarmingly precocious, Annie is a child mage with immense pyromantic power. Her favorite trick is summoning her beloved teddy bear, Tibbers, as a fiery protector.
